Thomson's e/m Experiment
Some additional information/fixed values
  • - The distance between the accelerating plates is 5 cm.
  • - Note that because the accelerating plates and deflection plates are connected in parallel, they have the same voltage across them.
  • - The inner diameter of the coil is 13.6 cm and the coils are 6.8 cm apart; each coil has 25 turns of wire, and the coil thickness is assumed to be negligible.
  • - The field is assumed to be uniform as taken from Wikipedia (it yields a more accurate result than extending the Biot-Savart result from along the central axis).
  • - The coils are hooked up to their power source in such a way that current travels counterclockwise (relative to our viewpoint) when the voltage is positive.
  • - The length of the deflection plates is 10 cm.
  • - The default settings, if nothing is changed, is as indicated in the placeholders for each value.
