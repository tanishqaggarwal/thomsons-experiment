Thomson's e/m Experiment
Some additional information/fixed values
  • The distance between the accelerating plates is 0.1 m.
  • The inner radius of the coil is 7 cm and the outer radius is 9 cm.
  • This simulation uses the Hemholtz coil field approximation given on Wikipedia as being applicable for the entire region between the two plates, e.g. it does not rely on Biot-Savart.
